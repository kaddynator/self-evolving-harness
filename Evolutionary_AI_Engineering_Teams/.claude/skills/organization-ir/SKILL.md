## Purpose
Work with the Organization Harness IR.

## Instructions
1. Treat the IR as the source of truth.
2. Include agents, prompts, tools, communication graph, runtime policies, failure recovery, evaluation, weakness mining, and mutation policy.
3. Keep the IR executable, versionable, inspectable, mutable, and regression-testable.
4. Do not mutate protected components such as task success conditions or validation gates.
5. Keep examples readable for hackathon judges.
