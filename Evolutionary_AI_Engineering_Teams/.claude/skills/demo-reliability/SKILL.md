# Demo Reliability Skill

## Purpose
Keep the hackathon demo stable and understandable.

## When to use
Use before changing anything that affects the demo path.

## Instructions
1. The demo must work offline or with mock mode.
2. Prefer deterministic tasks with tests.
3. Avoid relying on long-running agent loops.
4. Show evolution clearly:
   - Team V1
   - Failure or inefficient result
   - Mutation
   - Team V2/V3
   - Better result
5. Keep the final demo under 3 minutes.

## Demo success line
We are not optimizing answers. We are evolving AI engineering organizations.
